All about KDrama!

Basic info

Korean dramas are television series, often of the romance and drama genres, produced in South Korea. They usually consist of a limited number of episodes and are aimed at a wide audience.

famous actors

Lee Min-ho: Known for his roles in "Boys Over Flowers," "The Heirs," and "Legend of the Blue Sea."
Kim Soo-hyun: Gained fame through "My Love from the Star" and "It's Okay to Not Be Okay."
Park Seo-joon: Popular for his performances in "What's Wrong with Secretary Kim" and "Itaewon Class."
